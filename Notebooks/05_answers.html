Our model has some limitations. Firstly, we are using pricing information from other resorts to train our model. Since there is no guarantee that other resorts are making correct market prices, our model cannot guarantee that the result is optimized in the market.Secondly, it won't be sure that the total number of vistors and ticket purchasing number of each of them will be the same as our assumptions if we make some changes in our facilities. 


To fully understand the potential revenue change, we need to estimate the cost of a run (personnell, maintainance, electricity, etc.).


Another thing worth noticing is that our price is already among the highest in the state, we might need additional data in the Monata state to check other possible factors of price. A possible choice would be a statewise market survey.
